About Hannah Reyes

Your Trusted Partner in Austin Real Estate

Hannah Reyes brings 20 years of customer service experience and 9 years of dedicated service to Austin and surrounding areas as a real estate professional with Ascend Group LPT. She specializes in guiding first-time home buyers, seasoned investors, and families through every step of the home buying process with expert care and attention to detail.

Hannah believes that buying a home is more than just a transaction—it's about securing your future. Her expertise in negotiation ensures that clients not only find their ideal homes but also maximize their investment opportunities. Her low-pressure, compassionate approach empowers clients throughout the process, while her meticulous attention to detail ensures no aspect of the purchase is overlooked.

With nine years of Austin market expertise, Hannah provides local insights and guidance that make navigating the real estate landscape a streamlined experience. She is passionate about helping people build wealth through homeownership, offering personalized education and support to help clients achieve their real estate goals.

Hannah's commitment to delivering exceptional service and securing the best outcomes for her clients has established her as a trusted advocate in the Austin real estate market. Whether you're purchasing your first home or building an investment portfolio, Hannah provides the informed guidance and professional support needed to achieve homeownership with confidence.
