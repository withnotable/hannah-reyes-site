Neighborhood GuideAustin, TX

Mueller

Mueller transforms a former airport into a thoughtfully planned community with parks, trails, and local gathering spaces in East Austin.

A Planned Community on Historic Grounds

Mueller sits on the site of the former Robert Mueller Municipal Airport, transformed into a master-planned community that opened in the mid-2000s. This East Austin neighborhood represents one of the largest urban redevelopment projects in the United States, built with intentional design principles that emphasize connectivity and green space.

Architecture and Housing Character

The neighborhood features a diverse mix of architectural styles, from modern condominiums and townhomes to single-family residences with contemporary and traditional design elements. Many homes incorporate sustainable building practices and energy-efficient features, reflecting the community's environmental planning principles.

Parks and Green Space

Mueller is designed around extensive green infrastructure, including numerous parks, trails, and open spaces woven throughout the community. The neighborhood features a central green corridor and multiple pocket parks that create gathering spaces for outdoor activities and community events.

Local Gathering Spots

The community includes mixed-use areas with local businesses, cafes, and retail establishments that serve as neighborhood focal points. These commercial districts are designed to be walkable and accessible, creating spaces where residents can conduct daily activities within the community.
